English practise | Bandymas - anglų k. (testas nr. 1)

The test consists of two parts:

• listening task (25 minutes)

• speaking task (30 minutes)

The total duration of the test is 55 minutes. After the time expires, you will no longer be able to complete the tasks.

The total number of points for the test tasks is 40.

To continue, click Continue

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Please read all the information provided here carefully, as you will not be allowed to return to this window.

Do not refresh this window as the progress and results of this test are not saved.

You will be automatically moved to next tasks and submission after time expires and you will not be able to return to the previous task.

Some tasks may not fit in the computer window, so review the task using the vertical scroll bar or your computer mouse.

The remaining test time will always be displayed in the upper right corner of the screen, next to your name.

The following tools may be used during the test: a draft (provided by the teacher), a pen, a pencil, an eraser, highlighters, water bottle.

During the test, the use of smart devices (including mobile phones) and other external devices is prohibited.

During the test, if necessary, you may go to the toilet for no more than 5 minutes, but the remaining time is not interrupted. The teacher must be informed about the exit, and will note this information in the execution protocol.

The test will be performed in full screen mode. Disabling full screen mode during the test is prohibited.

The system records violations. If the number of possible violations exceeds the limit, the test may be terminated.
